Interaction Foundations – Fall 2023

Recipe Page

I designed a recipe page for my favorite chocolate chip cookies made from Betty Crocker's recipe. This was my first experience making a fully responsive webpage for both desktop and mobile layouts, and I am very happy with how it turned out. Next time I make these cookies, I will use this webpage instead of the Betty Crocker website!

Microsite - Designer, Developer

Recieving guidance from my Creative Director, Jay Won, I designed a website that encapsulates the rap singer, Daniel Caesar. The idea was to redesign his Wikipedia page to be its own site. I not only learned about Daniel Caesar during this process, but I also learned how to make a multi-page site. I am proud of this work!

Microsite - Creative Director

For the longest time, I have had an obsession with Volkswagen buses. Directing Jane Zhang on how to make a site that encapsulated the different models and history of this bus model was very fun. My hope was for the site to be fun, bold, and colorful, very unlike the typical car websites seen today. I think Jane did an excellent job of encapuslating this look, especially with the timeline!
